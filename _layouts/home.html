Hello!

I'm Elizabeth Case. I'm a scientist, writer, artist, and educator. Currently I'm working on my PhD at Columbia University and the Lamont-Dohety Earth Observatory. I'm the co-founder of Cycle for Science, an outreach program that translates science into hands-on K-12 lessons. I'm dedicated to building communities in the overwhelmingly white geosciences for all those who have been historically excluded through work like co-facilitating a seminar on Race, Climate Change, and Environmental Justice. My writing and audio work has been published in Scientific American (see here and here), the State of the Planet , and more.
